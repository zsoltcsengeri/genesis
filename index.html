DISCOVER THE NEW GENESIS G70

DESIGN

REDESIGNED AND REINSPIRED.

The new G70 sets out to make every drive memorable with a new, more aggressive front end, sharpened silhouette and a progressive rear. Reborn as the most athletic sport sedan of the Genesis lineup, the new Genesis G70 features upgraded style to match its performance.

VIEW DESIGN GO TO DESIGN

EXTERIOR

Distinctive styling takes shape in the new G70, featuring a lower and wider front end, new stacked quad LED headlights and sharper lines that dominate the exterior. With a focus on elegant athleticism, G70 proves simple can be elegant. The G70 is a high-performance sedan with an exterior design that exhibits the most athletic characteristics of the entire Genesis lineup and an interior that focuses on driver enjoyment.

INTERIOR

A high-tech cluster display, seats that wrap comfortably around you, and luxurious aluminum finishes combine for a truly remarkable interior experience.

GENESIS INFOTAINMENT SYSTEM (10.25")

An all-new 10.25" HD display features a GUI (graphical user interface) developed exclusively for Genesis and offers an optimal wide viewing angle for the driver.

NAPPA LEATHER & ALUMINUM ACCENTS

Carefully stitched Nappa leather and real aluminum finishes invigorate the driver’s senses. The new G70’s premium materials and dynamic interior layout elevate the joy of driving to the next level.

G70 3.3T SPORT EXTERIOR DESIGN

The new G70 3.3T Sport boasts exclusive dark-chrome daylight opening (DLO) moulding, as well as front radiator grille surround moulding, and dark tinted chrome mesh to accentuate the model's unique characteristics. The Sport model is further distinguished via 19" sport wheels and exclusive red Brembo® brake calipers to complement the new G70's staggering high-performance image.

G70 3.3T SPORT INTERIOR DESIGN

The signature red-stitched seats of the new G70 3.3T Sport boast a distinctive red colour and premium quilting patterns. Just sitting on them can provide a thrilling experience. Spin hairline-patterned aluminum materials on the door trims and centre console add a touch of elegance, and a metal gas pedal completes the Sport-inspired interior.

SAFETY

UNCOMPROMISING SAFETY

The advanced safety and driving technologies of the new G70 provide added confidence and comfort so that you can focus on enjoying the drive.

VIEW SAFETY GO TO SAFETY

INTELLIGENT FRONT-LIGHTING SYSTEM

The Intelligent Front-Lighting System (IFS) allows the driver to travel with their high beams on without the risk of obstructing the vision of oncoming traffic or any preceding vehicles. This function is achieved using LEDs with individual electronic controls that are automatically turned up or dimmed down as required.

8-AIRBAG SYSTEM

New centre side airbags have been strategically placed in the front seats to prevent injuries that may arise in the event of side collisions due to driver-passenger or occupant-object impact within the cabin. A total of eight airbags further protect all vehicle occupants, including advanced airbags for the driver and front passenger seats that detect the presence of a passenger and the severity of a collision to control deployment, side and curtain airbags for both the front and rear, and a driver's knee airbag.

FORWARD COLLISION-AVOIDANCE ASSIST (FCA, JUNCTION TURNING)

The new G70 warns and automatically assists in braking as needed if the preceding vehicle suddenly slows or if there is a high risk of a front-end collision due to a stopped vehicle or sudden appearance of pedestrians or cyclists. Braking assistance is also applied when the risk of a collision with an oncoming vehicle is detected when turning left at an intersection.

BLIND-SPOT COLLISION-AVOIDANCE ASSIST (BCA)

When BCA detects an imminent collision from the backside, while making a lane change or leaving a parking lot from the car’s parallel-parked position, it sends an alert and, if the risk of collision increases even after the alert, the BCA assists in avoiding the collision through automatic brake activation.

REAR CROSS-TRAFFIC COLLISION-AVOIDANCE ASSIST (RCCA)

RCCA warns the driver of approaching traffic from the left and right sides of the vehicle during reverse manoeuvres. Even after the warning, if necessary, it applies emergency braking to prevent a collision.

HIGHWAY DRIVING ASSIST (HDA)

Based on judging the distance from a leading vehicle on highways, and utilizing lane information and navigation data, the HDA assists the driver by controlling the driving speed to maintain a safe distance from other vehicles, and to prevent the vehicle from moving out of its lane.

Technology

ENGINEERED FOR ENJOYMENT

G70’s array of cutting-edge technologies prioritize a convenient and responsive drive.

VALET MODE

A new privacy protection feature that limits access to the user’s personal information when using a personal driver or parking service*.

*Available with Genesis Connected Services.

WIRELESS SMARTPHONE CHARGING

Effortlessly charge your Qi-enabled devices with the convenience of an available built-in wireless charging pad. If the driver exits from the vehicle, leaving the smartphone inside, an alert is sounded.

LEXICON® 15-SPEAKER SYSTEM

Experience exceptional sound with 15 premium Lexicon® speakers and Quantum Logic® surround sound technology. Optimally positioned for the best stereophonic sound, the speakers are paired with stainless steel grilles for crisp and clear projection. Supported by subwoofers under the front seats, the new G70’s audio system offers rich bass and an immersive listening experience.

HEAD-UP DISPLAY

An available Head-Up Display helps reduce distraction by displaying critical driving information on the windshield, directly below the driver’s line of sight.

PERFORMANCE

DYNAMIC ENGINEERING

Overwhelming and sporty driving performance revs up
your driving experience to the next level. The new G70
achieves the perfect driving balance between dynamism
and stability with technologies that actively respond to
changing road conditions and driving situations.

AWD + LSD INTEGRATED CONTROL SYSTEM

This system controls the standard all-wheel drive (AWD) transfer in tandem with the left/right traction control of the available mechanical Limited Slip Differential (mLSD), improving driving performance during turns. It also allows the driver to drift around corners*.

*Mechanical Limited Slip Differential (mLSD) available on 3.3T Sport models only.

SPORT+ MODE

The new Sport+ drive mode is geared for the track and will help you get the most out of the available 365-hp twin-turbo engine and sport-tuned suspension for an unforgettable driving experience.

BREMBO® BRAKING SYSTEM

3.3T G70 models are equipped with discernible Brembo® brakes that offer superior braking performance and an unparalleled sport aesthetic.

ELECTRONICALLY CONTROLLED SUSPENSION

An Electronically Controlled Suspension delivers a delightful balance of stability and comfort. COMFORT offers the stability of a luxury sedan, SPORT provides a dynamic and reliable ride, and SPORT+ offers an excellent sense of unity with the road.

VARIABLE EXHAUST TIPS

Depending on the selected drive mode (Eco, Comfort, Custom, Sport, Sport+, Smart), the active exhaust sound varies according to the accelerator pedal (opening of the throttle) to provide enhanced exhaust notes that best reflect the driver's performance preferences*.

*Available on 3.3T Sport models only.

POWERTRAIN

The new Genesis G70 is anchored by the incredible power of its engine, delivering an incredibly athletic driving experience in every way possible.

2.0 TURBO GASOLINE ENGINE

A low-to-mid-speed twin-scroll turbocharger, electric CVVT and continuously operating VCM generates high torque even at low speeds. Balances drivability and silence to maximize the driving pleasure.

MAX POWER 252 HP @ 6,200 RPM
MAX TORQUE 260 lb-ft @ 1,400-4,000 RPM
